What is special about Ram Mandir ?

The construction incorporates special bricks known as "Ram Shilas," with the inscription "Shri Ram" on them. These bricks draw a symbolic parallel with the stones believed to have been used in the construction of Ram Setu, connecting the modern craftsmanship of the temple with ancient symbolism....


What is Ram Mandir made of ?

High-quality Rajasthani sandstone, Makrana marble, and precious metals like gold and silver are being used, contributing significantly to the overall cost.


Why is Ram Mandir important in India ?

Why is Ram Mandir important in India? Devotees believe the temple site is Ram's birthplace. It was formerly occupied by a 16th century mosque razed in 1992 by Hindu extremists. That act of destruction prompted a wave of deadly Hindu-against-Muslim riots and helped fuel the rise of the so-called Hindu nationalist movement...
